About Brinkmann

Ms. Lindsay Brinkmann is a social studies teacher at DeLand High School, teaching awesome classes on American History among other historical periods. She connects with her students to build a successful learning environment and builds a faithful following that's lead to the creation of the Brinkmann 2020 campaign. Ms. Brinkmann started this campaign to do a great service to the world. By enacting strict, just laws and demanding high standards from society, she hopes to change the world for the better. In her spare time, she enjoys sewing quilts and watching Netflix.
